# Research Context

**Posture:** Read more than you write. Produce written findings, not just answers. Document what you learned and what you didn't.

## Key questions

- What do we know for certain?
- What don't we know?
- What are the options?
- What are the tradeoffs of each option?
- What's the cheapest experiment to reduce uncertainty?

## What to avoid

- Jumping to implementation before understanding the problem
- Premature recommendations without exploring alternatives
- Ignoring contradictory evidence
- Treating one source as authoritative without cross-checking

## Output format

Written finding with:
- **Context** — what was the question, why does it matter
- **What we found** — facts, with sources
- **Options** — at least 2, with pros/cons
- **Recommendation** — one option, with stated tradeoffs
- **Open questions** — what we still don't know

## Done means

Written finding exists with all sections. Options compared. Recommendation has explicit tradeoffs. Open questions listed for follow-up.
